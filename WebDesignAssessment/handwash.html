Proper Handwashing

To properly wash hands, follow the instructions from above.


Washing hands is one of the most important process when it comes to basic hygiene. It is recommended to was h your hands regularly and should be done when:
  • Before handling food
  • After going outside
  • Before eating food
If there is no access to water and soap, you can use a hand sanitiser or an anti-bacterial hand rubbing alcohol
